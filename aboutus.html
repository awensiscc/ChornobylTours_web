Discover the Untold Stories of Chernobyl

Want to see a place where time has stopped? A trip to the Chernobyl Exclusion Zone is an opportunity to touch history, walk along the streets of abandoned Pripyat, see the legendary Ferris wheel and feel the special atmosphere of this unique place. You will have:

  • Visit to Pripyat – explore empty houses, a school and the Lazurny swimming pool.
  • Nature of the Zone – see how nature reclaims the city from man.
  • Radar "Duga" – a giant structure from the Cold War.
  • Safety is guaranteed – the trip takes place along proven routes with a professional guide.

Myths and Reality of Chernobyl

Chernobyl is surrounded by rumors and legends. Some say that mutants live in the Zone, others say that nature has gained new strength here. We will help you figure out where the truth is and where the fiction is. Find out how radiation affected the environment, why some people returned to their homes and what the abandoned ghost town hides.

What does Chernobyl look like at night?

During the day, the Zone seems abandoned and silent, but with the onset of darkness, it takes on a completely different character. Moonlight shines through broken windows, the wind plays in empty houses, and the starry sky above Pripyat amazes with its purity. A night visit is a completely new look at this place.
